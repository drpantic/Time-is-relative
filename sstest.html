Subjective second test

Time is relative! One's subjective second may not be same as the real (objective second). Time perception and perceived duration of events depend on many physiological and psychological factors. This simple test allows the user to compare his subjective second with actual (real, objective) second. Click on the "Begin test!" button to start the test. One second later (your subjective second), click the "End test" button. Repeat this test at least 10 times. After that, click the "Show result" button. The page will display the average real length of your "subjective second".
